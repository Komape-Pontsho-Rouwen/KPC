Katz Plumbing & Construction Website 

Project Overview 

A professional and responsive website for Katz Plumbing & Contruction based in Limpopo,Moletjie.It is built to advertise their work online ,showcase completed projects,gain exposure and facillitates communication with clients.

live demo : https://komape-pontsho-rouwen.github.io/KPC/

Features: 

Front-end responsive website with home,services,gallery ,about us and contact us pages.

Home Page - Has clear company header with a motor and and two links styled as buttons to quickly take you to other sections of the same website and a picture of workers animated to grab user's attention.

Services Section - displays each service inside cards with icons and descriptions, allowing users to quickly understand what the business offers.

Gallery Section(not yet functional)-

On large screens: displays 6 images arranged 3 per row.

On small screens: displays 1 image at a time, with arrow navigation to move forward or backward through the gallery.

About Us Section -Contains a clear description of the company to build trust and credibility with users. It also includes a picture of the founder to personalize the brand and show authenticity.

Contact Us -Displays business address details and includes a contact form integrated with Formspree for direct client inquiries

There is also a call on action (not yet functional)

Appears only after leaving the Home page. It stays visible while navigating through the Services, Gallery, and About Us sections, encouraging users to take action.
It disappears when on the Home page and Contact Us page to avoid distraction and maintain a clean layout.

Tech stack 

HTML5 - For the structure and layout
CSS - For styling and responsiveness
JS - For interactivity when clicking buttons mainly in the gallery section(still under construction)
FormSpree - Handles form submitions from contact us page 
Git/Github - Version control and deployment of the website.
